StreamVault is a universal streaming media player that lets you organize and play all your video sources in one place — live channels, Plex libraries, IP cameras, local files, network shares, and more.

Built for Fire TV and Android devices with full D-pad remote navigation. No accounts, no tracking, no ads — your library stays on your device.

FEATURES

ITV — Live Channels
Browse and play live streams from M3U/M3U8 playlists or JSON repositories. Set locality priorities to put your regional channels first. Automatic failover switches to an alternative stream when one goes down. Import from URL or load locally.

EPG Guide
Full electronic program guide with a scrollable timeline grid, now/next info, and per-channel program details. Supports XMLTV sources. EPG data is parsed in the background so you can start watching immediately.

Live Sports
Live scoreboard integration powered by ESPN data. Game cards show real-time scores, status, and start times. Filter by your favourite leagues and teams with a My Teams mode. Supports NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL, MLS, soccer, college sports, and more.

PlexStr — Plex Integration
Connect to your Plex Media Server and browse libraries directly inside StreamVault. Resume where you left off. No separate Plex app needed.

iCam — IP Cameras
View RTSP, ONVIF, RTMP, and SRT IP camera streams. Scan your local network to discover cameras automatically. Add cameras manually or from scan results.

DVR & Timeshift
Schedule recordings and route them to a remote device over FTP. Timeshift lets you pause and rewind live streams for up to 30 minutes without stopping playback.

Local Media
Browse and play video files stored on your device. Add multiple folders and scan your device for video files automatically. Supports MP4, MKV, AVI, MOV, and TS.

Network Media
Stream from Samba/SMB shares and FTP servers on your local network. Add multiple sources and manage credentials securely.

HDHomeRun
Discover and stream from HDHomeRun network tuners. Full guide data pulled directly from your device.

Repository Browser
Browse stream repositories with region and category filters. Add your own repo URLs. Streams are cached locally for offline access.

PRIVACY
StreamVault stores all configuration locally on your device. No telemetry, no cloud sync, no accounts required.
